Our Story

It is a pioneer in providing a unified platform to bridge the gap between parents, teachers, educators and students in order to bring them on a single page for an exchange of healthy information related to the field of education and activities which will aid to theconstructive development of kids.

Our elementary objectives include:

  • To bring parents, teachers and students on one platform for better communication and discussions pertinent to education and extra co-curricular activities of kids.
  • To discuss all kinds of education systems prevailing these days in schools of Karachi.
  • Vigorous discussions for proficient parenting and schooling
